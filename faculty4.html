As the Dean, I am delighted to welcome you into this prestigious Faculty, where we strive for excellence and growth. The Faculty’s partnership with one of the most prestigious universities in Europe and the world, TU Dublin University, gives graduates a competitive advantage in the labor market. We fully appreciate the importance of your decision to choose the Faculty of Finance and Management, which represents an investment in your future. Why Study Business at PUA?

1- Dual Bachelor’s Degree: Our college offers a unique opportunity to obtain two prestigious academic degrees. The first is a Bachelor’s Degree in Financial and Administrative Sciences, accredited by the Supreme Council of Universities in Egypt . The second is a Bachelor’s Degree in Management, awarded by TU Dublin in Ireland, renowned as one of the world’s oldest and esteemed universities, established in 1592.
2- International Student Exchange: Our college offers a prestigious and enriching international student exchange program. Spend a semester studying abroad, immersing yourself in a different culture, and gaining a global perspective.
3- Path to Advanced Studies and Postgraduate Degrees: Our College of Business Administration offers a seamless pathway to pursue master’s and doctoral studies at TU Dublin in Ireland. Transition effortlessly from your undergraduate studies at PUA to advanced degree programs at TU Dublin without the need for Equivalency certificate.
4- Industry-Relevant Curriculum: Our college is committed to providing a curriculum that aligns with the needs and demands of the industry. We continuously update our courses to incorporate the latest trends, technologies, and best practices in the business world. By studying with us, you will gain practical skills and knowledge that are highly relevant and applicable to real-world business environments.
5- Extensive Networking Opportunities: Networking plays a crucial role in career development. Our college offers numerous networking opportunities, connecting you with industry professionals, alumni, guest speakers, and fellow students. These connections can open doors to internships, job opportunities, mentorships, and collaborations, setting the stage for a successful career.
6- Supportive Learning Environment: We prioritize creating a supportive learning environment that fosters growth, collaboration, and innovation. Our dedicated faculty members are experienced professionals who are passionate about teaching and mentoring students. Additionally, we provide access to resources, workshops, and extracurricular activities that enhance your personal and professional development.
7- Strong Alumni Network: Joining our business college means becoming part of a strong and influential alumni network. Our graduates have gone on to excel in various industries and sectors, both locally and internationally. Being part of this network provides ongoing support, mentorship, and potential career opportunities throughout your professional journey.
