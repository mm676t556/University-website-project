Job Vacancies

PUA aims to appoint highly qualified full-time academic staff members who meet the following requirements:

Academic Rank:

  • Faculty Members: Professors, Associate Professors, and Lecturers who hold a PhD with an equivalent international qualification in their field of specialization; sufficient years of experience in the academic field, an extensive record of quality publications, and a track record in leading research activities.
  • Assisting Staff: those who hold MSc or PhD in their field of specialization.

Professional Skills:

  • Excellent command of the English language both spoken and written is a must.
  • High level of competence in planning and delivering lectures, seminars, and tutorials.
  • Ability to foster and develop study and research skills among students.
  • Comprehensive computer skills (ICDL is an advantage).
  • Multicultural experience.
  • Teamwork skills involving expanding the new university and establishing new departments.
  • Employ basic interpersonal skills to communicate with both students and colleagues from different cultural backgrounds.

Information about Working at PUA:

  • Salaries are dependent on academic qualifications and professional experience.
  • PUA is an equal opportunity employer.
  • The language of instruction is English.
  • The University offers attractive salaries with additional fringe benefits.

To apply online, download and fill:
1 - Academic Staff Application Form (Professor, Assistant Professor, Lecturer)
2 - Academic Staff Assistants Application Form (Teacher Assistant, Demonstrator)
and send to: hr.acad@pua.edu.eg
Photocopies of certificates must be attached.

To apply for administrative vacancies at the university, please send your CV to the following E-mail: hr.administrative@pua.edu.eg
Please mention the position you will apply for in the E-mail subject.
