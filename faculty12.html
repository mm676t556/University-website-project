Faculty of Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence

“Welcome to the Faculty of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence! In this dynamic and rapidly evolving field, we explore the intersection of technology, innovation, and human potential. Computer science forms the foundation of our digital age, enabling us to develop software, algorithms, and systems that power our modern world. Artificial intelligence, on the other hand, represents the forefront of innovation, as we teach machines to simulate human intelligence and decision-making. As you embark on your educational journey here, you’ll delve into the intricacies of coding, algorithm design, data analysis, and machine learning. These disciplines not only empower you to create cutting-edge technologies but also equip you with problem-solving skills applicable across industries. The fields of computer science and artificial intelligence are gateways to diverse and exciting careers. From software engineering to data science, from robotics to virtual reality, the opportunities are boundless. Our graduates find themselves at the forefront of industries, transforming healthcare, finance, entertainment, and beyond. The advancements in AI and computer science have already altered the way we live, work, and interact. From autonomous vehicles to personalized medicine, from intelligent virtual assistants to climate modeling, the impact is far-reaching. However, with great power comes great responsibility. As future professionals in this field, you’ll play a pivotal role in shaping the ethical, social, and economic dimensions of these technologies. The decisions you make in the development and deployment of AI systems will influence our privacy, security, and overall well-being. At our faculty, we emphasize not only technical excellence but also the cultivation of critical thinking and ethical reasoning. We encourage you to consider the implications of your work, engage in multidisciplinary collaborations, and promote the responsible and equitable use of technology. In essence, the world of computer science and artificial intelligence is both a canvas for innovation and a realm of profound influence. As you embark on this journey, remember that your contributions have the potential to change lives, empower industries, and shape the future. Welcome to a realm where imagination meets computation, and where the boundaries of what’s possible continue to expand.”

Prof. Dr. Saad Darwish

Dean of Faculty

Why Study Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence at PUA?

  • Developing competencies in the field of computer science and artificial intelligence, specifically in the field of data mining and information retrieval using developed methods. Moreover, the application of modern technological methods and smart apps in the field of proper management and decision-making as it is considered one of the most important topics that have been at the forefront of scientific research recently.
  • The faculty of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence qualifies graduates to work in large companies that depend on modern and digital technology.
  • Providing scientific knowledge and gaining professional experience in many fields such as physics, robotics and mathematics.
  • Graduates are awarded a bachelor degree that helps open up new scientific horizons.
