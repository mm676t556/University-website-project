WELCOME NOTE FROM THE PRESIDENT OF PHAROS UNIVERSITY

I take pride in being the President of Pharos University PUA , this ancient university founded in 2006 as Alexandria’s first private university. Working for PUA is like no other, as it offers constructive cooperation in a family atmosphere with a distinguished group of professors, administratives, and students. I work diligently to serve everyone within this giant educational institution that offers a distinguished and diverse educational service. PUA also seeks to realize the UN Sustainable Development Goals , and Egypt 2030 Strategy. Read more
