Faculty Of Physical Therapy Dean Welcome

Prof. Dr. Amir Nazih Wade’a

My Dear Students, On behalf of the Faculty of Physical Therapy family—including our esteemed faculty members and dedicated administrative team—I extend my heartfelt wishes for your success, carrying with it the hope of a bright and promising future. Together, we take pride in this distinguished scientific institution, which continues to excel across all its departments. Our faculty has worked diligently to achieve a prestigious standing and a strong reputation, striving to be a leader in education, scientific research, and community service. This is accomplished by fostering an academic environment that nurtures creativity, entrepreneurship, excellence, and innovation. We firmly believe that everyone within the faculty—management, faculty members, teaching assistants, administrative staff, and students—is part of one family, united by mutual respect, collaboration, dedication, and a shared commitment to achieving the college’s vision, mission, and strategic goals, which align with those of the university. The faculty plays a vital role in advancing Egypt’s educational system, empowering students with the tools for systematic thinking, and cultivating their ability to critique and analyze scientifically. We leave no stone unturned in inspiring a love for knowledge and fostering a sense of responsibility among our students. The faculty serves as a platform for democratic dialogue, objective discussions, and a correct understanding of scientific principles, while also strengthening the bond between students and their professors. Moreover, the college remains committed to providing a forward-thinking educational environment characterized by a wide cognitive horizon. This is reflected in our use of modern electronic communication systems and the comprehensive services offered by the college library. We also place great emphasis on nurturing student talents, encouraging participation in various activities, and supporting the youth of our college through tailored initiatives. I have full confidence in your collective efforts and absolute trust in your ability to contribute to the betterment of the faculty community. May Allah guide us all on the path to success and prosperity, and may He protect our beloved Egypt—its land, people, and leadership—from all harm and evil. And Allah is the Grantor of All Success.

Reasons to Study at PUA’s Faculty of Physical Therapy

  • Implementing a strong regulation compliant with regional and international specifications and standards related to physical therapy.
  • Providing spacious classrooms equipped with the latest educational methods.
  • Equipping anatomy, chemistry, physiology, and other laboratories with all devices that serve the practical aspect.
  • Using multiple modern and interactive educational methods and aids that increase student understanding.
  • Using an interactive electronic educational platform that continuously connects students with their lecturers.
  • Providing practical training during academic years in most of the Ministry of Health hospitals in Alexandria.
  • Providing the educational clinics with the latest diagnostic and therapeutic equipment to serve the educational process and community.
  • Hiring faculty members and teaching assistants with the highest knowledge, teaching skills and responsibility.
  • Providing continuous academic advising to students to solve all their academic, social and other problems.
  • Using a continuous student evaluation system to ensure the provision of top-tier educational quality.
  • Organizing various student activities to develop the student’s spirit of cooperation and social integration.
  • Holding an annual faculty conference in which local and international scientists and researchers participate to expand students’ and graduates’ awareness of the latest trends in physical therapy.
  • Continuously holding workshops and seminars to serve students and graduates.

Career Opportunities

Graduates of the Faculty of Physical Therapy have multiple career opportunities, including, but not limited to:

  • Sports clubs medical staff.
  • Physical therapy departments in private hospitals.
  • Nursing homes.
  • Rehabilitation centers.
  • Traveling, especially to the United States of America, Australia, Europe, and the Arab Gulf.
  • Opening up to two private physical therapy centers and have them licensed from the Ministry of Health and Population.
  • Completing the internship period in the hospitals of the Ministry of Health and Medical Insurance.
